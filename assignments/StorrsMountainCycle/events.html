"Cycle the Continental Divide"

Come join us for the experince of a lifetime! Take a twelve day trip along the Continental Divide along dirt roads and mountain passes and enjoy spectacular views and terrain unlike anything you have seen before! Sign up today!

Dates: July 5th-16th

Route: Polaris, MT to South Pass City, WY, 510.4 miles

Skill Level Required: Experienced/Expert

Costs: $2,500 (all permits included)

Basic Requirements:

  • Tubeless tires with a minimum of 3oz sealent per tire
  • Minimum of 2.25" width tires with low-profile tread and thick sidewalls
  • All weather camping gear
  • High-quality water filter (one per person)
  • Rain gear
  • All meals for the entire trip
  • Transportation to and from the beginning/end of the route to your home
